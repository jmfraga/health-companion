## Draft v1 — 168 words

**Health Companion**

The health system today gets paid when you get sick. Hospitals, visits, pharmacies — every actor is paid to treat disease. Nobody is paid to keep you well. Eric Topol described this inversion in 2015; the tools to close it did not yet exist. With Opus 4.7 they do.

Health Companion is a wellness product whose only job is to keep people well, in the language they actually speak. It never diagnoses, never prescribes, always refers. It is a warm, patient clinical voice authored and reviewed by a practicing physician — me — that remembers what matters, surfaces the conversation at the right moment, and hands the user a structured record their own doctor can read.

We built it in five nights with a coordinated team of Claude Code subagents. One product, two surfaces ahead: a companion in the patient's pocket and, next, a record on the clinician's desk.

*Open-source. Apache 2.0. github.com/jmfraga/health-companion.*
